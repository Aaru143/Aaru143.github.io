Background :


 For our porject we have used the data collected by FBI(Federal Bureau Of Investigation) under its UCR(Uniform Crime Reporting) Program. The objective of this program is to collect reliable information for use in law enforcement administration, operation, and management; over the years. Over the years, this data has become one of the country's leading social indicators. Through the UCR Program, the FBI publishes the statistics required by data users. Typical representation include alphabetical tabulations of crime data for states, metropolitan statistical areas, cities, counties, colleges and other agencies.

Understanding the data :

 For each year, we referred 5 types of tables as listed above. Here is a description of our the fields used from each table for this project :
  • Number of Males Arrested (Age Distribution)
    • Offence Charged: Lists the type of offense
    • Total All Ages: Lists the total number of people arrested corresponding to each offense
    • Ages Under 18: Lists the total number of people that were below 18 and were arrested for the corresponding offense
    • Ages 18 and over: Lists the total number of people that were or above 18 and were arrested for the corresponding offense


  • Number of Females Arrested (Age Distribution)
    • Offence Charged: Lists the type of offense
    • Total All Ages: Lists the total number of people arrested corresponding to each offense
    • Ages Under 18: Lists the total number of people that were below 18 and were arrested for the corresponding offense
    • Ages 18 and over: Lists the total number of people that were or above 18 and were arrested for the corresponding offense


  • Total Number Of People Aressted
    • Offence Charged: Lists the type of offense
    • Total All Ages -> Year: Lists the total number of people arrested corresponding to each offense for that particular year


  • Number Of People Aressted (by Race/Ethnicity)
    • Offence Charged: Lists the type of offense
    • Percentage Distribution -> Race/Ethnicity: Lists the total percentage of people arrested corresponding to each offense for that particular race/ethnicity. The included races are : White, Black/African American, American Indian/Alaska Native, Asian, Native Hawaiian/Other Pacific Islander


  • Number Of People Aressted in each State
    • State: Lists the name of the state
    • Total all ages: Lists the total number of people arrested in the particular state
